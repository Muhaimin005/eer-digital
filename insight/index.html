Strategic Partnership for Sustainable AI Infrastructure

EER Digital appointed as Authorized Reseller across Malaysia, Singapore, Vietnam, and the Philippines.

We are proud to announce that EER Digital has been appointed as an authorized reseller for VASCOM and BYD across Malaysia, Singapore, Vietnam, and the Philippines.

Through this collaboration, we bring to market a breakthrough AI GPU server solution designed for liquid immersion cooling, a revolutionary method where entire servers are placed in a special cooling liquid. This eliminates the need for traditional air-conditioning systems, dramatically reducing energy consumption and operational costs in data centers.

This partnership underscores our dedication to building energy-efficient, high-performance, and sustainable computing infrastructure, helping organizations modernize their digital operations while contributing to a greener planet.
