Account Manager (Sales Executive)

Location: Plaza Mont Kiara

Requirements

  • Diploma or Degree in Business, IT, or related fields (preferred, not mandatory).
  • 1–5 years of experience in sales, account management, or customer-facing roles.
  • Fresh graduates with strong communication skills are encouraged to apply.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Self-motivated, energetic, and able to work with minimal supervision.
  • Passionate about technology and eager to learn.
  • Possess own transport and willing to travel for client meetings.

Keys Responsibilities

  • Drive new business by identifying and engaging potential customers (“new logo acquisition”).
  • Manage and grow assigned customer accounts with regular engagement and relationship building.
  • Understand customer needs and work with the Presales team to propose suitable solutions.
  • Achieve assigned sales quota and contribute to overall team targets.
  • Prepare sales proposals, quotations, and presentations.
  • Provide regular updates on pipeline, forecasts, and customer activities to CCO.
  • Ensure customer satisfaction and act as the main point of contact for any issues.
  • Participate in events, networking, and digital outreach to build company’s brand visibility.

Preferred Attributes

  • Exposure to IT/software solutions (SaaS, digital products, services) is an advantage.
  • Strong work ethic, good attitude, and a team-first mindset.
  • Goal-oriented and driven to build a career in tech sales.

Pre-Sales Engineer

Location: Plaza Mont Kiara

Requirements

  • Diploma or Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, IT, Engineering, or a related field.
  • 3–5 years of experience in presales, solution consulting, or technical roles within IT/software.
  • Strong understanding of SaaS, enterprise software, and digital transformation solutions.
  • Experience with RFP/RFI responses, solution demos, and client presentations.
  • Excellent communication, documentation, and presentation skills.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities with attention to detail.
  • Able to work independently and collaborate closely with sales and technical teams.

Key Responsibilities

  • Work closely with the Sales and Account Management teams to understand customer requirements and propose suitable solutions.
  • Participate in the full presales lifecycle: requirement gathering, solution presentation, product demonstration, proof-of-concepts (POC), and RFP responses.
  • Prepare technical proposals, solution architectures, and supporting documents that align with EER’s offerings (SaaS, digital transformation, enterprise applications, etc.).
  • Collaborate with delivery and technical teams to validate solution feasibility and cost estimates.
  • Support the configuration and demonstration of software solutions to prospects and partners.
  • Assist in developing presentation materials, proposal templates, and reusable presales assets.
  • Keep abreast of industry trends, technologies, and competitors to continuously refine solution strategies.
  • Provide technical input for tenders and work closely with partners for integrated solution proposals.

Preferred Attributes

  • Experience in SaaS products (HRMS, e-invoicing, document management, analytics).
  • Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP).
  • Familiarity with digital projects in Malaysia or ASEAN markets.
  • Self-driven, proactive, and comfortable working in a dynamic, high-growth environment.

Administrative Executive (Admin / HR / PA)

Location: Plaza Mont Kiara

Requirements

  • Age below 35.
  • Minimum Diploma/Degree in Business Administration, Human Resource, Office Management, or equivalent.
  • 1–3 years of experience in Admin/HR/PA roles.
  • Experience in HR documentation, recruitment, or basic payroll is an added advantage.
  • Strong organisational skills for managing office operations and documentation.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint).
  • Able to handle scheduling, emails, letters, and memo preparation.
  • Possess own transport.

HR & PA Responsibilities

  • Assist with recruitment (job posting, screening, interview arrangements).
  • Manage staff attendance, leave records, and provide basic payroll support.
  • Prepare HR letters such as offer, confirmation, memo, and warning letters.
  • Maintain employee records and ensure HR SOPs are updated.
  • Support onboarding, training, and performance review activities.
  • Manage calendars, appointments, and travel arrangements for Director/Management.
  • Prepare meeting materials, minutes, and follow-up action items.
  • Maintain strict confidentiality of all company and personal matters.

Preferred Attributes

  • Good communication skills in English and Bahasa Malaysia.
  • Strong interpersonal skills and pleasant personality.
  • Highly organised, detail-oriented, and proactive.
  • Able to multitask, work independently, and handle tight deadlines.
  • Willing to work beyond regular hours when required.
